Meals
For Hope

The MOBI is the Zimbabwe's very first
Mobile soup kitchen, providing
nutritious meals and support to
woman and children in some of
our most vulnerable and isolated
communities.

The MOBI was launched in October 2021 and is already providing
its beneficiaries with crucial access to nutrition, support,
education, healthcare and long term change.

The MOBI is an initiative that aims to reduce hunger and poverty in women and children
with a focus

  • Food & nutrition
  • Health and psychosocial support
  • Eduaction and literacy
  • Disaster/emergency response and relief
  • Awareness, fundraising and support for homeless artisans
